Question 1 of 8

Question 2 of 8

Transitions, losses, breakthroughs, relocations, relationship shifts — anything that changed the terrain.

Places, people, dynamics, routines — where do you expand and where do you contract?

The thought that feels shameful, crazy, selfish, or unforgivable. You can be indirect. Encrypted hints work. This field holds without judgment.
